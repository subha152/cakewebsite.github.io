About Us

We at firstcrushstore.com delivers flowers,cake and gifts across all over India.Since its birth First Crush store has been devotedly making gift deliveries across pan India, offering the best, quickest and the most hassle-free services in the entire industry.

We are aiming to offer best value for money, best quality in product and services with a dedicated customer support.

Today First Crush Store is a one-stop-shop for your gifts and greetings. It offers the broadest handpicked selection of flowers, cakes, chocolates, fresh fruits, dry fruits, sweets and designer gifts.

The central office of First Crush Store based in Bangalore, with branches in all major cities of the country. We give utmost priority to customer service and customer satisfaction, which propels them to always provide on-time deliveries. Consignments sent through them are well packed, carefully handled and delivered on priority basis, every time.

We have trust and responsibility placed by thousands of happy customers, we also strive to make that special occasion for you and your loved ones even more special by delivering surprise gifts like cakes, chocolates, teddy bears and sweets.
